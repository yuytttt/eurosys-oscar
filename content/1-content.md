## Talk Abstracts

### Key Technologies of the Intelligence Boom

*Hu Xinwei*

An overview of the core technical advances driving the current AI revolution, with a focus on the systems-level implications for infrastructure, scalability, and future hardware-software co-design.

---

### The Aversion to Systematization in Systems Research

*Prof. Timothy Roscoe*

Systems research has long resisted rigorous systematization. This talk examines why — and asks whether the field's resistance to formalism is a strength to preserve or a limitation to overcome, particularly as AI begins to automate parts of the research process itself.

---

### How Will AI Systems Do Systems Research?

*Ant Rowstron*

AI is increasingly capable of writing code, running experiments, and reading papers. What happens when AI systems start doing systems research? This talk explores what that means for the field, for researchers, and for the kinds of problems we choose to work on.
